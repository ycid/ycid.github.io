Research

My main research interests lie in commutative algebra and algebraic geometry. I am primarily interested in questions that arise in geometric or combinatorial contexts and in understanding how their possible answers manifest in various algebraic structures or invariants. I often collaborate on problems at the interface of commutative algebra, algebraic geometry, and combinatorics. I like topics related to blow-up algebras, local cohomology, multiplicities, differential operators, integral closure, Hilbert schemes, symbolic powers, syzygies, regularity, and matroids.
